About the Workshop

IUCAA Centre for Astronomy Research and Development (ICARD), GLA University, Mathura is organizing HEAW-2025 during November 18-20, 2025. The purpose of the workshop is to provide opportunities to UG/PG students, Research Scholars to get an exposure on current issues in the High Energy Astrophysics and its implications in other research areas.

This workshop is sponsored by IUCAA, Pune.

About GLA University

GLA University, Mathura is a premier educational institution known for its excellence in education and research. Established with a vision to create world-class professionals, the university offers a wide range of programs in engineering, management, sciences, and humanities.

The IUCAA Centre for Astronomy Research and Development (ICARD) at GLA University is a dedicated research center focused on astronomical studies and research, collaborating with national and international institutions to advance our understanding of the universe.

With state-of-the-art facilities and a team of experienced faculty members, GLA University provides an ideal environment for academic growth and research innovation.

Topics to be Covered

Introduction to the Stars and Black holes

Fundamental concepts and recent discoveries in stellar astronomy and black hole physics.

Current issues in compact object astrophysics

Exploring the latest research and challenges in the study of neutron stars, white dwarfs, and other compact objects.

Computational issues in X-ray and Gamma-ray astrophysics

Data analysis techniques and computational challenges in high-energy astronomy.

Physics of Relativistic jets and Blazars

Understanding the mechanisms and phenomena associated with relativistic jets from active galactic nuclei.
